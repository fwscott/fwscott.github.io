Why travel organizers choose VoyaReach

Group communication without the group chat — private, simple, and built for mixed-connectivity travel.

Numbers stay private

Travelers never see each other’s phone numbers. Organizers don’t have to share their personal cell either.

Reaches travelers anywhere

Some travelers have Wi-Fi, some have local SIMs, some have neither. VoyaReach tries WhatsApp first and falls back to SMS automatically.

Zero app installs

Travelers receive messages on WhatsApp or SMS — they never need to download anything new.

QR check-in

Scan a code at departure. VoyaReach captures the right phone number for each traveler — no manual lists.

One message, every channel

Write once. VoyaReach figures out each traveler’s best channel and routes accordingly — no group chat to manage.

Delivery tracking

See who received each broadcast and through which channel — in real time, per message.

Not another WhatsApp group

WhatsApp groups expose every member’s phone number to every other member. That means travelers see your personal cell — and you see theirs. VoyaReach keeps every number hidden behind the platform. Organizers communicate without giving out personal contact info, and travelers keep theirs private too.
